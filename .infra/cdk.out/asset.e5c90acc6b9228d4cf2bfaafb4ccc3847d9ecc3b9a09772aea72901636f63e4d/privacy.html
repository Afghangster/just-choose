Privacy Policy

Effective: May 2025

1. What We Collect

  • Account info: email address, display name, age, gender
  • Connection data: who you're connected to, invite codes (temporary), connection aliases/nicknames, avatar images
  • Choices: the question text, option labels, option photos, who the choice was assigned to, the chosen answer, and any optional comment
  • Device info: push-notification token (only if you enable notifications)

2. What We Don't Collect

We do not collect location data, contacts, browsing history, advertising identifiers, health data, financial information, or message content outside of choices.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To operate the app: deliver choices, manage connections, show results
  • To send push notifications you opted into
  • We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third-party advertisers

4. Data Storage & Security

  • Data is stored in Supabase (hosted on AWS) with row-level security
  • Images are stored in Supabase Storage buckets
  • All communication uses HTTPS/TLS encryption

5. Data Retention

  • Account data is kept while your account is active
  • Deleted connections remove the link but keep past choices for both users
  • You can request full account deletion by contacting support@justchoooose.com

6. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — database, authentication, storage
  • Expo — push notifications
  • Apple Sign In / Google Sign In — authentication only; no data shared back

7. Children

Users must be 13 or older to create an account.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes means acceptance of the updated terms.

9. Contact Us

For privacy questions or account deletion requests: support@justchoooose.com
