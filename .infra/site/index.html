How It Works

Three steps. Five seconds. Done.

Skip the sign-up marathon. Bypass the awkward group chat. Get straight to the decision.

1
→

Connect

Link up with your partner, flatmate, or that one friend who can never decide. One invite code. Done.

→

Build a visual decision card with photos, prices, notes — whatever they need to stop stalling.

3

Decide

They tap their answer. You see the result. Crisis averted. Relationship saved. You're welcome.

Visual Decisions

Not just text.
Beautiful choice cards.

Add photos, prices, and notes to each option. Make the decision feel real — because "pizza or sushi" hits different when you can see both.

Cards are private between you and your connection. Leave the group chat drama behind. Your choices stay totally private.

Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

🔒

Totally Private

Decisions stay between you and your connection. Keep it totally private without social feeds or public sharing.

📸

Photo Options

Add images to each choice. Because "the blue one" means nothing without a picture.

⚡

Decision Lock

For urgent choices, opt-in to temporarily limit your own distractions until you answer. Self-controlled, always optional.

🎨

6 Themes

From warm Classic to sleek Monochrome to soft Pink Blossom — pick the vibe that matches your energy.

🔔

Push Notifications

They'll know the second you send a choice. They’ll be notified instantly, so nobody can claim they missed your text.

📊

Decision History

Every choice is saved. Perfect for when they say "you always pick the restaurant".
