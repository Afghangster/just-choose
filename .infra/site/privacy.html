Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 30, 2026

At Safain Labs Ltd. ("we", "us", "our"), we respect your privacy. Just Choose is designed to be a private tool for you and your connection. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how it's used, and most importantly, what we don't collect.

1. What We Collect

We only collect the essential data needed to make the app work:

  • Account Information: Your email address or authentication provider ID (used for login).
  • Profile Data: Basic demographics you choose to provide and your avatar URL.
  • Connections: Invitation codes and the status of your connection with your partner, friend, or family member.
  • Decisions (Choices): The options you create, photos you upload for choices, and the decision history between you and your connection.
  • Device Information: Push notification tokens (so we can alert you when a decision is made).

2. What We DON'T Collect

We believe your decisions are yours. We do not collect or track:

  • No Location Data: We never track or ask for your GPS location.
  • No Social Graphs: Outside of the single person you connect with via an invite code, we do not access your contacts, friends lists, or social media feeds.
  • No Sneaky Analytics: We don't use heavy third-party tracking or advertising SDKs to follow you across the internet. Just Choose is completely ad-free.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is strictly used to facilitate the core features of the app:

  • To deliver choice cards and responses to your connection instantly.
  • To send you push notifications when a decision is pending or resolved.
  • To maintain your personal decision history.

4. Data Deletion

You are in full control of your data. You can delete your account at any time directly within the app. Deleting your account will permanently remove your profile, connection links, and decision history from our active databases.
