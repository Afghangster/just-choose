# Privacy, Terms, and Account Deletion Outline

This is the MVP outline for legal copy. Replace with reviewed legal text before store submission.

## Privacy Policy Outline

Just Choose processes:

- Account identifiers from Supabase Auth.
- Profile display name, age, gender, and optional avatar.
- Connection records and invite codes.
- Private connection display names that users set for their own view.
- Decision cards, option images, notes, prices, links, boards, and responses.
- Push tokens and notification preferences.

## Account Deletion

The app must include an account deletion path from Settings > Safety and privacy.

Deletion should:

- Remove the profile.
- Remove push tokens.
- Remove notification preferences.
- Remove or anonymize decision content according to the final privacy policy and data retention rules.

## Terms Outline

- Just Choose is for private decision-making between connected people.
- Users must not use the app for coercion, harassment, surveillance, or unsafe situations.
- Users can leave a connection at any time.
- The service may be unavailable or platform-limited when permissions or entitlements are unavailable.
